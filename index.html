Biography

I am a 3rd Year Ph.D. in Computer Science at Stony Brook University, specializing in Computer Vision and Deep Learning. I am currently a Research Assistant advised by Prof. Zhaozheng Yin.

My research interests include 3D Vision & Robotics, Self-supervised Learning, Generative AI, and Multimodal Learning (Vision + X). I also apply machine learning to Medical Image Analysis .

I have spent two wonderful summers in Amazon Lab126, solving perception and manipulation problems in embodied AI, working on single image to 3D generation, language-guided affordance estimation, 6-DoF pose estimation & tracking, and view-point translation to make robots smarter and more adaptable.

Previously, I was a Data Scientist at Tata Digital, where I developed a visual search engine for fashion apparel. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Jadavpur University.
