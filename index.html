// Computer Vision & Deep Learning

Researching the visual intelligence of machines

I'm a 4th-year PhD student in Computer Science at Stony Brook University, advised by Prof. Zhaozheng Yin. My work spans 3D Vision, Self-supervised Learning, Generative AI, and Multimodal Learning, with applications in medical image analysis .

I've spent two summers at Amazon Lab126 tackling embodied AI — single-image 3D generation, 6-DoF pose estimation, language-guided affordance, and view-point translation for smarter robots .

Previously a Data Scientist at Tata Digital building visual search for fashion. B.E. in Electrical Engineering from Jadavpur University.
